About CryptoX

The Future of Crypto Trading (Demo Edition)

What is CryptoX?

CryptoX is a fully simulated cryptocurrency exchange platform built for learning and demonstration purposes. Experience the thrill of crypto trading without any real financial risk.

Features

  • Real-time order matching engine
  • Market and limit order support
  • Interactive price charts
  • Live order book visualization
  • Portfolio tracking
  • Recent trades history

Technology Stack

Built with pure vanilla JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3. No frameworks, no build tools, no dependencies - just clean, modern web technologies running entirely in your browser.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

This is a DEMO application. No real cryptocurrency trading occurs on this platform. All data, trades, and balances are simulated for educational purposes only. Do not use this as financial advice.
